Welcome to ICCET 2022

In view of Ongoing Pandemic situation, the Conference will be hosted in Hybrid or Virtual Mode.

ICCET-2022(International Conference on Computing in Engineering & Technology) is the 7th Edition of DBATU Conference Series organised by Department of E & TC Engineering. The previous version of the conference were held at:

  • ICCASP-2016 to 2018 & 2021: University Campus, Dr. B A Tech. University, Lonere-India.
  • ICCET-2019: Deogiri College of Engineering, Aurangabad-India.
  • ICCET-2020: MGMs College of Engineering, Nanded-India.
